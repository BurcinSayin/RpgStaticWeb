Fiefs of the Kingdom of Kalamar

This map shows the division of the fiefs within the Kingdom of Kalamar, along with the arms of the current lord of the fief. The list below details the names of the fiefs, the current holder of the title and the arms of the lord/lady. The fiefs are listed in order of precedence within the aristocratic hierarchy of the realm.
