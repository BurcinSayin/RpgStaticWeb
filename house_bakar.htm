Personal Arms of the Emperor of Kalamar

The Emperor's personal arms are the platinum lion of House Bakar (now the emblem of the Empire) surrounded by a grey border emblazoned with 20 crowns that represent the 20 kingdoms, grandduchies and archduchies that made up the old 'Vast Empire', for which the Emperor still claims overlord status.
