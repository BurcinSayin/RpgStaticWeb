Major Divisions of the Imperial Army of Kalamar

Im’perata et Kalamar – “Imperial Army of Kalamar" is the name given to the entire army of Kalamar, as commanded by the Gekata Im’peramada.

Im’pera Legata Gatasi – “The Guardians of the Imperial Throne” (or Imperial Guard Legions) is an honorific bestowed upon legions that have distinguished themselves in the service of the Emperor. Collectively they form a legionary army under the command of the Im’peramada et Im’pera Legata Gatasi.

Bron’kalo Gatasi – “Frontier Guards” is the legionary army responsible for guarding the Ka’Asa frontier. They are under the command of the Im’peramada et Bron’kalo Gatasi.

  

Organisational Structure of the Imperial Army of Kalamar

  

Organisation of a Legion (Legata)

Commander: Legatamada assisted by two Falikamada. These two Falikamada are considered to be the senior captains in the legion.

Units: Each Legata has a notional strength of 3,500 legionaries. The Legata is made up of seven Falikata: five heavy infantry Falikatas I-V; one cavalry Falikata VI; and one archer Falikata VII. Each Falikata therefore comprises 500 men. A Falikata is composed of five Tarisata, each of 100 men. A cavalry Falikata has three Tarisata of heavy cavalry (Taristas I-III) and two of light cavalry (Tarisatas IV-V). An Tarista has five 20 men Bolikata.
